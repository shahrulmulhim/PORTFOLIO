About Me

Dream to reality, reality to digitality.

Mulhim Design is a creative design initiative established by a Diploma in Architecture student from Ungku Omar Polytechnic, Perak — built around a strong passion for design and innovation, aiming to deliver ideas that are practical, functional, and visually inspiring.

I specialise in architectural drawings, concept development, and BIM-based design that prioritise functionality, innovation, and client satisfaction. Every idea is shaped with precision and creativity, turning visions into well-designed, buildable outcomes.

Through BIM technology, I ensure better visualisation, improved coordination, and detailed architectural documentation — helping clients understand their projects more clearly from the earliest design stages.
