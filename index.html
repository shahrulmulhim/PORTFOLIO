About Me

Architecture.
BIM. Innovation.

I am Muhammad Mulhim, a Diploma in Architecture student (Semester 6) at Politeknik Ungku Omar, Perak — consistently on the Dean's List with a CGPA of 3.77.

My 20-week industrial training at Salur Kuasa Digital Sdn Bhd, Petaling Jaya as a BIM Modeler gave me real-world exposure on landmark projects including KLIA QAQC and the Royal Museum (Muzium Diraja).

I specialise in the intersection of heritage architecture and digital construction technology — leveraging BIM, VR, and IBS modular systems to serve communities and preserve cultural memory.
