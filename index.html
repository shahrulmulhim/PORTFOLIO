About Me

Dream to reality, reality to digitality.

I am Muhammad Shahrul Mulhim bin Shahrul Azlan — an architecture student at Ungku Omar Polytechnic, Perak, consistently on the Dean's List with a CGPA of 3.79.

Skilled in AutoCAD, Revit, SketchUp, D5 Render, and Photoshop, with a strong interest in advancing my 3D modelling and BIM project coordination capabilities. I work between hand-drawn concepts and fully coordinated BIM documentation.

Beyond design, I bring leadership, attention to detail, and a collaborative mindset — shaped through competitions, community programmes, and five semesters of studio briefs.
