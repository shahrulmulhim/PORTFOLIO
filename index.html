About Me

Dream to reality, reality to digitality.

I am Muhammad Shahrul Mulhim bin Shahrul Azlan — an architecture student at Politeknik Ungku Omar, Perak, currently pursuing a Diploma in Architecture (2023–Present). I have achieved Dean's List for every semester with a CGPA of 3.79.

I am skilled in AutoCAD, Revit, SketchUp, D5 Render, and Photoshop, with a strong interest in learning more about Revit to enhance my 3D modelling and project coordination skills. I also work with Navisworks, Civil 3D, Enscape, and ArcGIS for BIM coordination and digital construction workflows.

Through BIM technology, I ensure better visualisation, improved coordination, and detailed architectural documentation. I am passionate about sustainable and innovative architecture, and I am willing to relocate and travel for work opportunities.

Academic Background: Previously at SM Tahfiz Darul Ridzuan, Daerah Manjung, Perak (2018–2023) where I achieved SPM 7A with Distinctions in Malay Language, Business, Science, Modern Mathematics, Pendidikan Syariah Islam, Al-Quran AS-Sunnah, and Hafazan Al-Quran. I am also a Hafiz in Al-Quran (30 Juz) and served as School Prefect (SPBT).
